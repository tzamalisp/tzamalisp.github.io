About Me

Pantelis Tzamalis received his Ph.D. at the University of Patras, focusing on the Artificial Intelligence of Things domain. His doctoral work developed an innovative set of mechanisms and platforms composed of Human Activity & Gesture Recognition, Trustworthy AI, Explainable AI, Crowdsensing, and Text Mining applications for passive, automated, and effective detection, evaluation, and monitoring of Allergic Rhinitis at spatiotemporal and individual scale.

He also received his M.Sc. in the Division of Application and Foundation of Computer Science and Technology (2018), and his Diploma in Computer Science (2014) from the Computer Engineering and Informatics Department, University of Patras, Greece.

He has worked for Google in the context of Google Summer of Code 2019 and 2020, developing open-source software for Machine Learning infrastructure in collaboration with JBoss (Red Hat) and MetaBrainz.

His publications span international conference proceedings and journals, and he serves as a reviewer for various international conferences and scientific journals.
