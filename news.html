Invited talk to ITU's Digital transformation Webinar Series

On November 2, 2021, I was invited το the International Telecommunication Union (ITU)’s webinar series on Digital transformation for cities and communities. ITU specifically is the United Nations specialized agency for information and communication technologies – ICTs.

My talk was about how AI and IoT technologies can be merged and provide healthcare solutions in the domain of Healthcare 5.0. In particular, I presented my work progress over the last 5 years and how the applications that my team and I developed are able to identify and monitor a disease exacerbation. The invited talk had the name "Exploiting the Power of the Crowd for the efficient monitoring of diseases exacerbation". The presentation can be found available for download at this link.

In general, this webinar discussed the underlying principles of crowdsourcing, requirements for crowdsourced systems, as well as highly innovative applications in smart cities and communities. It brought together experts to discuss the role of crowdsourcing and crowdsourced systems in the verticals of Smart Manufacturing and Digital Health. The webinar also placed​ a unique emphasis on the role of ITU international standards in facilitating IoT-related based crowdsourced systems in smart cities.
